Results Format
Select the option that most closely reflects the desired format of the results.
  • Particle Concentration - The ‘Particle Concentration’ option corresponds to results representative of the number of particles within the home.
  • Integrated Exposure - The ‘Integrated Exposure’ option corresponds to results representative of a resident’s cumulative exposure to particles within their home due to a visitor.
